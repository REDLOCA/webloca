Welcome,

The International Workshop on Locational Analysis and Related Problems took place on
September 27–29, 2017 in Segovia (Spain).

It was organized by the Spanish Location Network and GELOCA, the SEIO working group on Location.
GELOCA belongs to the Spanish Society of Statistics and Operations Research (SEIO).

The Spanish Location Network is composed of more than 100 researchers, distributed among several Spanish universities, and has been funded by the Spanish Government.

The aim of the workshop is to promote communication among researchers and to contribute to the development of locational analysis and related problems.
The previous meeting was held in Málaga (September 14–16, 2016).

The topics of interest include location models, networks, transportation, logistics, exact and heuristic solution methods, and computational geometry.

Researchers and practitioners were invited to present works in the form of oral communications or posters.
For participation, the submission of a short abstract was required.
