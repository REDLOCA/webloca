Welcome,

The XI International Workshop on Locational Analysis and Related Problems was held in Elche (Spain) from January 31st to February 1st, 2022.

This workshop was organized by the Spanish Network on Location and Related Problems and GELOCA, the SEIO working group on Location. These organizations bring together Spanish researchers focused on locational analysis and related problems, organized into six thematic nodes across several Spanish universities.

This edition received financial support from the Generalitat Valenciana through project PROMETEO/2021/063.

The topics of interest include location models, networks, transportation, logistics, exact and heuristic solution methods, and computational geometry.

Researchers and practitioners were invited to present works in the form of oral communications or posters.
For participation, the submission of a short abstract was required.
